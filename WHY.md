# Why TEREO

TEREO is not mainly about writing code.
It is about deciding what was real.

`tereo` is the runtime.
TEREO is the proof layer built around it.

## One Line

`AI writes. TEREO proves.`

## Instinct

`Doubt first. Keep later.`

## Law

Gain has to beat noise.

This is the center.
Everything else exists to make that law usable.

## Baseline

Each kept receipt becomes the next baseline.

TEREO does not chase spikes.
It raises the floor.

A `hold` keeps fake gains from raising it.
That is how small, true gains stack.

## Why AI Fits

AI is very good at producing changes.
That makes proof more important, not less.

The more powerful the generator becomes, the more valuable the judge becomes.

## Why The Shape Is Small

The front door should fit in your head:

- promise
- check
- receipt

The depth should live behind the front door:

- repeated runs
- control reruns
- confidence
- dogfood

TEREO should not make you remember every cautious move.
It should doubt automatically when a change looks promising but not proven yet.

## Why It Feels Different

Most tools try to help you make more changes.
TEREO tries to help you keep fewer, truer changes.

That difference matters.

TEREO also treats `hold` as a first-class answer.
Not yet proven is different from false.

Its main artifact is not the progress chart.
It is the receipt.

Its summary view is not an analysis notebook.
It is a proofboard: current frontier, confidence, and recent receipts in one place.

## Philosophy

- compression beats sprawl
- proof beats vibes
- doubt first
- keep later
- AI writes
- TEREO proves
- one good-looking run is a clue
- repeated good runs are evidence
